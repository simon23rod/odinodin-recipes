Peanut butter cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of shortening
  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup of honey
  • 2 room temperature large eggs lightly beaten
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Baking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

    2. In a bowl, mix shortening, peanut butter and honey.

    Add eggs, mix well.

    Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder and salt, add to peanut butter mixture and mix well

  2. Roll into 1-11/2-in balls and place om ungreased baking sheets.

    Flatten with a fork dipped in flour.

    Bake unil set (about 8-10 min). Remove to wire racks to cool

    Enjoy!
